Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results from my ads? +

Most clients see initial results within the first 7–14 days. Significant ROI improvements typically occur within 30–60 days as we optimize campaigns based on data.

Do I need a large budget to start? +

No! We work with budgets of all sizes. Our Starter package begins at Rs. 15,000/month (management fee), plus your ad spend which can start as low as Rs. 5,000–10,000.

What industries do you work with? +

We work across all industries — retail, hospitality, e-commerce, restaurants, real estate, healthcare, education, and more. If you have a business in Nepal, we can grow it.

Will I have access to see my ad performance? +

Absolutely. Complete transparency is our standard. You'll receive weekly/monthly reports and can request access to live dashboards at any time.

Can you work with my existing social media pages? +

Yes! We work with your existing pages and accounts. You retain full ownership of all pages, ad accounts, and data at all times.
